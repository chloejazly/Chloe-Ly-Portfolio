UCSD's Alpha Clinic

As the only student representative on UCSD's Alpha Clinic Community Advisory Board, I am informed about the clinical trials they have conducted and are currently in progress from the point of view of both doctors and former patients. While expanding my knowledge, I get to voice my thoughts and share my perspective on how stem cell therapy can be made more accessible to a broader audience, such as college students like myself.

First Gen Scholars

As a designer for First Gen Scholars, I worked with other first-generation students and staff to highlight the stories of first-generation students through designing flyers and advertisements. Additionally, I created infographics for First Gen Scholars' social media pages to feature announcements about scholarships and events. Every week, I attended Zoom meetings which alternated between board and club sessions, where I shared my ideas and gained insight from other board members. UCSD's SPARK

As one of ten students in UCSD's Summer Program to Accelerate Regenerative medicine Knowledge (SPARK), I had the opportunity to intern at the Jiang Lab, a regenerative medicine research lab, while also exploring careers in the field. During my time as a research intern, I had hands-on practice growing Jurkat cells by conducting cell culture, while also performing other lab procedures like RT-qPCR and RNA extraction. Outside of working hours in lab, I participated in workshops geared towards enhancing practical skills in medicine such as CPR on infants and adults, suturing, and taking vital signs. Additionally, I was responsible for creating and presenting a scientific poster at CIRM's annual conference in Oakland and the program's closing ceremony at UCSD.

Project

The project, MediMatch, is an app designed to connect users with various volunteer opportunities in the healthcare field. Through the app, users can explore different types of healthcare volunteering roles and gain insight into each position's details and requirements. If users are interested in applying for a specific role, they can easily do so by clicking the "Apply Here" button or save the position for future consideration by clicking the save banner icon. For users unsure where to begin, a quiz is available to match users to possible suitable volunteering opportunities. This front-end project was developed using Figma which was used to design graphics, organize elements, and create an interactive experience for users through its prototyping tools.
